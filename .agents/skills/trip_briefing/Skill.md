# Task

1. **Destination Information**: Gather and present key details about the destination, such as local culture, language, currency, and popular attractions.
2. **Travel Arrangements**: Outline the travel itinerary, including flight details, accommodation information, transportation options, and any necessary documentation (e.g., visas, travel insurance).
3. **Important Tips**: Offer practical advice for the trip, such as packing recommendations, health and safety tips, local customs to be aware of, and any COVID-19 related guidelines.
4. **Emergency Contacts**: Provide a list of emergency contacts, including local emergency services, the nearest embassy or consulate, and any relevant contact information for the traveler.
5. **Weather Forecast**: Include a weather forecast for the destination during the travel dates to help with packing and planning activities.

You can use tools in ./.agents/tools to gather the necessary information for the trip briefing. Make sure to customize the briefing based on the specific needs and preferences of the traveler.
